About The Icon Salon

Where beauty, confidence, and style come together.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, The Icon Salon began with a vision: to create a space where beauty meets artistry. Starting with a single branch in Manila, we grew with the trust of our clients who believed in our passion for exceptional hair care and styling. Today, The Icon Salon is known as a trusted name for those who want to look and feel their best. Our journey is fueled by creativity, skill, and the desire to make every client shine with confidence wearing their crown.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide high-quality hair and beauty services that not only enhance our clients’ appearance but also boost their confidence. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels valued, cared for, and beautiful.

Our Vision

To be the most recognized and trusted salon brand in the Philippines, known for our innovation, customer care, and passion for helping clients express their unique style and personality.
